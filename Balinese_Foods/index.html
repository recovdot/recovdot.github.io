Menu of the week

Babi Guling

Babi guling is a food made from piglets whose stomachs are filled with spices and vegetables and then roasted while rotating until cooked.

Rp. 1.200.000

Ayam Betutu

Ayam betutu is a food made from chicken meat that has been cleaned and then smeared with Balinese spices all over the surface of the chicken meat, then roasted in a husk fire.

Rp. 85.000

Lawar Plek

Lawar Plek is Lawar made without vegetables but only using raw pork and some Balinese spices. Lawar Plek is very delicious eaten with a mixture of raw pepper and white rice.

Rp. 28.000

Jukut Ares

Jukut Ares is a type of vegetable soup made using banana saplings and filled with Balinese spices, namely megenep base.

Rp. 30.000
